Description: This prefab allows you to add an awesome audio management system right into your game!

The Audio Management System is designed to centralize and simplify the handling of various audio elements in Unity. It provides a flexible way to manage background music, sound effects, and other audio clips with features such as fade-in/fade-out, looping, and volume control.

The audio management system includes the following:

  • Looping of background music
  • Fading out of background music
  • Custom length to play audio
  • Code written in Object Oriented C#

Features:

  • Loop(): Enables looping for the background music.
  • FadeOut(float duration): Starts the fade-out process over the specified duration.

Requirements: Unity 2022.3.42f1 or later
